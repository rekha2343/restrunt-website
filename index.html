Food Menu

Curd Rice

Curd rice, also known as "thayir sadam" in South India, is a traditional dish made by mixing cooked rice with yogurt (curd) and then seasoned with mustard seeds, curry leaves, and other spices. It is a cooling and soothing dish, often served as a part of a meal or eaten on its own, especially during hot weather.

Price: ₹ 50

Special Dosa

Dosa is a popular South Indian crepe made from fermented rice and urad dal (black gram) batter. It is thin, crispy, and golden-brown in appearance, typically served with coconut chutney and sambar

Price: ₹ 50

Kaju Rice

Kaju rice, also known as cashew rice or kaju pulao, is a flavorful and aromatic Indian rice dish made with basmati rice, cashew nuts, and various spices. It is often garnished with fried cashews and served with raita (yogurt-based side dish) or a curry.

Price: ₹ 60

Poori

Poori, also known as puri, is a deep-fried Indian bread made from unleavened wheat flour dough. It puffs up beautifully when fried, creating a light and fluffy texture inside and a crispy golden-brown exterior

Price: ₹ 50

Idli

Idli is a traditional South Indian steamed rice cake made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal (black gram). These soft, spongy cakes are a popular breakfast item in India and are enjoyed with coconut chutney and sambar

Price: ₹ 50

Tomoto Rice

Tomato rice, also known as "thakkali sadam" in South India, is a flavorful and tangy rice dish made by cooking rice with a spicy tomato-based sauce. The dish is infused with a blend of aromatic spices, tomatoes, onions, and sometimes vegetables, creating a delicious and comforting one-pot meal

Price: ₹ 50
